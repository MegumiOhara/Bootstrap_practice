Choose our recipes based on the Pepper Scale!

We have made recipes with different peppers and you can choose based on three different spciciness based on the Scolville Scale. The Scoville scale rates the spiciness of peppers through the measurement of capsaicin levels. Created by Wilbur Scoville, the unit of measurement is the Scoville Heat Unit (SHU), and chili peppers range from 0 SHUs (bell pepper) to over 2 million SHUs.

Basing on the Scolville scale, we have separated the choices to Mild, Medium, Hot, Super Hot
